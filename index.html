Medical Device Design · Ninewells Hospital
Soft Robotic Colonoscopy Device

Designed and developed mechanical concepts and prototypes for a soft robotic colonoscopy device under the supervision of Dr. Luigi Manfredi at Ninewells Hospital, Dundee. Applied iterative design methodology using SolidWorks CAD, FDM/SLA/SLS additive manufacturing, and vacuum forming. MSc Thesis · University of Dundee
3D Printing of Human Organ Models

MSc thesis investigating the 3D printing of non-biological human organ models for surgical planning and medical training applications. Utilised SimpleWare and MIMICS software to process medical imaging data (CT/MRI) and generate accurate 3D geometries for additive manufacturing. Explored material selection, dimensional accuracy, and clinical utility of printed anatomical models.

SimpleWare MIMICS CT/MRI Processing Additive Manufacturing Biomedical

BE Final Year Project · Materials Science
Chromium Interlayer on Pack Borided Steel

Experimental investigation into the effect of a chromium interlayer on the microstructure and microhardness of pack borided steel. The study analysed how chromium deposition prior to boriding influences surface hardness, boride layer morphology, and diffusion behaviour — with implications for improving wear resistance in industrial components.

Materials Science Microhardness Testing Surface Engineering Boriding SEM Analysis
